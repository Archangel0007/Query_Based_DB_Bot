This database consolidates all eCommerce order-related data into a single table, including information about the customer, product, shipping, and order status. It captures essential details like customer contact info, product specifics, order quantities, and total prices, including taxes and discounts. Payment and order statuses are tracked to facilitate the management of order fulfillment and customer interaction. It also stores promo codes and sales taxes for promotional analysis and reporting. Each entry represents a complete order from product selection to shipping, ensuring a holistic view of sales and customer behavior. This non-normalized structure is designed for ease of querying and analysis, making it suitable for reports or data extraction.
